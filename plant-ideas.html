What Can I Plant?

Here are some crops that have been successful in the past. Click on the photos for more planting information.

When to plant: May-August

When to harvest: as early as 6 weeks after planting

Learn more about growing basil.

When to plant: March-September

When to harvest: 60-80 days after sowing seeds or up to 4-5 months

Learn more about growing carrots.

When to plant: May-July

When to harvest: for slicing cucumbers, harvest when they are 7"-9" long

Learn more about growing cucumbers.

When to plant: May-June

When to harvest: when the outside color is a glossy purple, the fruit is firm and before the seed changes color

Learn more about growing eggplant.

When to plant: October-November

When to harvest: July

Learn more about growing garlic.

When to plant: May-Aug

When to harvest: pods of shell peas should be rounded and still have a glossy sheen

Learn more about growing peas.

When to plant: May-Aug

When to harvest: typically when they are green and the walls are firm and have thickened

Learn more about growing peppers.

When to plant: May-Aug

When to harvest: usually from 37 to 45 days after seeding, but can take much longer to mature during late fall and winter months

Learn more about growing spinach.

When to plant: May-Aug

When to harvest: it is recommended that tomatoes be harvested at the breaker to turning stage

Learn more about growing tomatoes.
